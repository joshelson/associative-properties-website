About Associative Properties

Where creativity meets precision in the heart of the Rocky Mountains

Founded in 2016, Associative Properties has been at the forefront of design innovation across Colorado and Utah. We specialize in creating harmonious spaces that reflect the unique character of the Mountain West while incorporating cutting-edge design principles and sustainable practices.

Our team brings together expertise in interior design, architectural consulting, project management, and creative direction. We work with residential clients, commercial developers, and hospitality brands to deliver exceptional design solutions that exceed expectations.

Our Design Process

A collaborative approach that ensures your vision becomes reality

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and project requirements. This includes site visits, budget discussions, and timeline planning.

02

Concept Development

Our team creates initial concepts, mood boards, and design proposals that align with your vision and budget. We present multiple options for your consideration.

03

Design Refinement

Based on your feedback, we refine the design, select materials, and create detailed specifications. This phase includes 3D renderings and material samples.

04

Implementation

We manage the entire implementation process, coordinating with contractors, overseeing installations, and ensuring quality control throughout the project.

05

Final Reveal

We present your completed space with final styling and photography. Our relationship continues with post-project support and maintenance guidance.
